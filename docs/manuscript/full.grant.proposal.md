## Risk Management & Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|------------|----------|
| Lower-than-expected ΔT or flow | Medium | Iterative prototyping with sensor feedback; fallback to staged linear design |
| Labyrinth clogging / durability | Medium | Modular tunnel sections; regular maintenance protocols; material additives testing |
| Biomass yield variance | Low | Conservative 35 tons/acre target; diversified fuel sources (algae + waste) |
| Regulatory / permitting delays | Medium | Start with small prototype on private land; engage local authorities early |
| Safety incidents | Low | Comprehensive safety manual, PPE requirements, third-party review of critical components |
